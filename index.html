Agile, innovative and iterative.

This is how we deliver.

1 - Introduction Call

All good things start with a conversation. An introductory call helps us mutually decide if we are a good fit and align on high-level objectives. It's also your chance to ask us any questions.

2 - Discovery Session

This is where we really dig into your business challenges, users, priorities and goals to define what success looks like, timelines and deliverables. We spend a lot of time listening here.

3 - Design Sprint

Working with our UX and UI partners, we conduct thorough user research and prototyping exercises to ensure our solution is viable, scalable and ultimately adding value. This gives us confidence that we are building the right solution.

4 - Development Sprint

Taking our clearly defined scope and validated concepts, we follow agile delivery processes to translate wireframes and process flows into fully functioning applications. We thoroughly test our code on every release

5 - Support & Maintenance

All software continues to evolve after launch. We work with clients on an ongoing basis to iteratively define, design and build new features and functionalities, alongside offering on-going maintenance and support packages.

6 - Handover & Training

We are able to support the platform's transition to an internal team, conducting a comprehensive handover process to ensure complete continuity. We can also provide on-going training and mentoring for your development team.
